Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Overview

The No.JS documentation site (no-js.dev) is an open-source project. We respect your privacy and collect only minimal, anonymous data to understand how the documentation is used and improve it over time.

What data is collected

When you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID G-566FC80W9J) to collect anonymous usage data. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Device type, screen resolution, and browser
  • Approximate geographic location (city-level, derived from IP address)
  • General interaction events (scrolls, clicks on navigation)

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information. Google Analytics anonymizes IP addresses before storage.

Why we collect it

We use this data solely to improve the documentation site:

  • Understand which pages and sections are most useful
  • Identify pages with high bounce rates that may need improvement
  • Decide which content to prioritize or expand
  • Ensure the site works well across browsers and devices

Cookies

The following cookies may be set on your device:

Cookie Purpose Duration
nojs_consent Stores your cookie consent preference (1 = accepted, 0 = declined) 1 year
_ga Google Analytics: distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics: maintains session state 2 years

Google Analytics cookies are only set if you click Accept on the consent banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are stored.

How to opt out

You can control analytics collection in several ways:

  • Cookie consent banner — Click "Decline" when prompted. To change your choice, clear the nojs_consent cookie from your browser and reload the page.
  • Browser Do Not Track (DNT) — While not universally honored by all services, we respect your intent to limit tracking.
  • Google Analytics Opt-out — Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Browser settings — Block third-party cookies or use your browser's private/incognito mode.

Third-party services

The documentation site loads resources from the following third-party origins:

  • Google Analytics (googletagmanager.com) — Usage analytics (consent required)
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — Web fonts (Space Grotesk, Inter, JetBrains Mono)
  • jsDelivr (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — CDN for the No.JS framework script

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

Data retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. We do not export or store analytics data outside of Google Analytics.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since this is an open-source project, all changes are tracked in the GitHub repository.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub Issues.
